Scan a QR Code Online — Step by Step

Follow these simple steps to read any QR code instantly without an app.

1

Choose Your Scanning Method

Select Webcam Scanner to scan QR codes using your device camera in real time, or Upload Image to scan a saved QR code image or screenshot from your device. You can also paste an image URL directly.

2

Point Camera or Upload Image

For webcam scanning: click Open Camera, allow browser camera permission, and point your camera at the QR code. For image scanning: drag and drop the image or click to browse and select your file.

3

Get Instant Results

The scanner automatically detects and decodes the QR code. The result appears immediately in the results panel, including the QR type (URL, WiFi, contact, etc.) and the decoded content.

4

Copy, Open, or Save the Result

Click Copy Text to copy the decoded data to your clipboard. If it's a URL, click Open URL to visit it. All scans are automatically saved to your local history for later reference.
