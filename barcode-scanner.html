Free Online Barcode Scanner

Our barcode scanner lets you read product barcodes, inventory codes, and shipping labels directly from your webcam or by uploading an image no dedicated barcode scanner app required. Whether you need to decode an EAN-13 barcode from a product package, scan a Code 128 barcode from a shipping label, or verify a UPC-A code on retail packaging, QRCodeScan handles it all from any modern web browser. Built on Google's ZXing multiformat barcode detection library, our Qr Code Scanner supports over 10 barcode symbologies and provides instant results with format identification. All scan history is automatically saved and can be exported as CSV for inventory management, price comparison research, or retail workflows.
