About Us

We believe essential QR code tools should be free, private, and accessible to everyone no account, no data collection, no limits.

Our Mission

QRCodeScan was built with a simple mission to provide the most reliable, fastest, and most privacy-respecting QR code tools available online completely free. Whether you're a business owner creating QR codes for your marketing materials, a traveler trying to connect to hotel WiFi, a developer testing QR integrations, or simply someone who received a QR code and wants to decode it, QRCodeScan has the right tool for you.
